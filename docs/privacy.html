What We Collect

This extension collects media playback data from video and audio players on web pages (play/pause status, volume, current time, duration) and sends it to your Home Assistant server. You provide the server URL and access token.

How We Use It

We use this data only to send media information to Home Assistant and to receive playback controls from Home Assistant. That's it.

Who Gets Your Data

Only your Home Assistant instance gets your data. We don't share it with anyone else, sell it, or use it for ads.

Storage

Your settings (URL and token) are stored locally on your device. All communication is encrypted (WSS connection).
